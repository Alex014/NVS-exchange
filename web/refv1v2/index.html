Howto exchange your v1 COIN-HOURS to v2 NESS

#1 Open your wallet

Addresses

Locate address where you had most coin-hours in v1 network (Token address) (in our case urgGHeuHmczm9Zg2CLCVVHb7YD2EEH8mom)

Locate or create new empty address (Payment address) (in our case MJBH4MBxybBkRJPk4KMqKuuwiFpJYbJukj)

Go to NVS exchange

Enter previously located Token address and Payment address

Pay for slot with token - 1

You can pay with EMC, NESS and Coin-Hours

The cheapest way is to pay with COIN-HOURS (which are generated)

You need only 1 COIN-HOUR and any amount of NESS (0.000001 is enough)

Pay for slot with token - 2

Go to your wallet

Go to [Send] TAB

Swith to [Advanced] mode

Uncheck [Use automatic Coin Hour allocation]

Enter destination address and Coin-Hour ammount

Pay for slot with token - Payed

Wait ~ 1min and click [Confirm money send]

If transaction is confirmed you will see the next page ...

Now you must wain 10min+ for Exchange to accept your token inside Emercoin NVS blockchain

Payment data to check your v1 address

If Exchange has found your token and your token data is correct than your token will be accepted by Exchange and marked as Activated.

You will be prompted to send any amount of NESS from [Token address] to [Generated address] to check if [Token address] realy belongs to you.

Payment from your wallet

Go to your wallet

Go to [Send] TAB

Swith to [Advanced] mode

Find and check [Token address] from addresses list

Uncheck [Use automatic Coin Hour allocation]

Enter destination address and Coin-Hour ammount (1) and minimum NESS amount (0.000001)

The exchange has paid your token

Press [Reload] btn

If everything is OK you will see next page ...
